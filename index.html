SELFIELATION

Trespass into a different dimension and explore a collection of quarantine selfies and sounds—and the story behind them—put together in an interactive atmosphere that mimics the chaos that 2020 was. This installation has been created to highlight the individual experiences lived in the pandemic and their different, yet relatable angles.

An art project by Ellen Gustavsson, Elton Gutoch, Emely Morales Rosales, Kimberly van der Jagt, Linn Granström, & Milena Buchenhorst.
