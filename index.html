SELFIELATION

Trespass into a different dimension and explore a universe where the collective suffering of isolation is put into perspective in an immersive audiovisual experience. This installation has been created to highlight the individual experiences lived in the pandemic and their different — or related — angles.

An art project by Ellen Gustavsson, Elton Gutoch, Emely Morales Rosales, Kimberly van der Jagt, Linn Granström, and Milena Buchenhorst.
